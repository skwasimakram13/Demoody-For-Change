About Demoody For Change

Demoody For Change is a social initiative founded by entrepreneur Sk Wasim Akram, driven by the belief that businesses have the power to create positive social impact.

We pledge 5% of profits from all our businesses to build a better tomorrow through systematic social work, environmental conservation, and community welfare programs.

Founder's Vision

"Success is not just about profit margins, but about the positive impact we create in society. Through Demoody For Change, we transform business success into social change."

- Sk Wasim Akram, Founder
